\# Retail Customer Churn \& Revenue Intelligence



\## Business Overview



RetailHub is an online retail company that sells gifts, home décor, office accessories, and seasonal products to customers across multiple countries.



Over the past year, management has observed that revenue growth has slowed while repeat purchases have declined. Although new customers continue to join, a significant number fail to make repeat purchases, resulting in increased customer acquisition costs and reduced long-term profitability.



The leadership team wants to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify churn patterns, evaluate product and geographic performance, and develop strategies to improve customer retention.



\---



\## Business Problem



The company lacks visibility into:



\- Customer purchasing behavior

\- Revenue trends

\- Customer retention

\- Churn patterns

\- High-value customer segments



Management wants data-driven answers instead of assumptions.



\---



\## Business Objectives



The project aims to answer the following questions:



\### Revenue



\- Is revenue increasing or decreasing over time?

\- Which months generate the highest revenue?

\- Which countries contribute the most revenue?

\- Which products contribute the highest revenue?



\### Customers



\- Who are the most valuable customers?

\- Which customers purchase only once?

\- Which customers purchase frequently?

\- What is the average customer lifetime value?



\### Churn



\- How many customers have churned?

\- Which customer segments churn the most?

\- How much revenue is lost because of churn?

\- Which countries experience the highest churn?



\### Product Intelligence



\- Which products drive repeat purchases?

\- Which products have declining sales?

\- Which categories should receive marketing attention?



\### Business Recommendations



Provide actionable recommendations that can help:



\- Increase customer retention

\- Improve repeat purchases

\- Increase revenue

\- Reduce customer churn
