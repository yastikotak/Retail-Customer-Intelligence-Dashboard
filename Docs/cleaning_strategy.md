\# Cleaning Strategy



\## Rule 1



Problem



Cancelled invoices have negative quantities.



Decision



Keep them for return analysis but exclude them from revenue analysis.



Reason



They represent genuine business events.



\------------------------------------------------



\## Rule 2



Problem



Missing CustomerID



Decision



Investigate before removing.



Reason



May represent guest purchases.



\------------------------------------------------



\## Rule 3



Problem



Duplicate Rows



Decision



Verify duplicates before removal.



Reason



Could represent repeated transactions.
